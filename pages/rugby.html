Rugby World Cup

Description of event

The Summer Olympic Games, also known as the Games of the Olympiad.
This is often referred to as the Summer Olympics. It is a major international multi-sport event normally held once every four years.
The inaugural Games took place in 1896 in Athens, Greece.
The most recent Games were held in 2021 in Tokyo, Japan.
